About Us


  • Our Story

    • This is a company that was founded in 2021 by a partnership consisting of a former employee of a admin clerk at the City of Cape Town in the planning department as well as a former City of Cape Town member and a renowned architect who put their expertise into this business. They measure sites, draw up plans consisting of sub divisions, alterations to properties, either commercial or residential.


  • Mission statement

    • Mission is to design and ensure the safe and building of infrastructure that supports all legal regulations at the correct prices allocated to the plan according to the amendments made on the plan and its majority and minority.


  • Vision

    • Vision of the business (D & A Sub Company) is to expand the brand of the business and to grow the company into a national business market that can support multiple home owners and business owner.
